    FAQ

    What does ANTBOW mean?

    ANTBOW means "Box" or "chest" in the Ancient Kemetic (Egyptian) language system.

    Where are the buttons?

    This instrument is meant to be an all inclusive buttonless sound manipulator, triggered by keyboard, mouse, or touch inputs. A model utilizing "drumpad" style buttons is coming soon!
